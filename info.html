mackenzie pringle info

mackenzie is a designer based in san diego, working across digital product design, brand identity, and art direction.

he’s currently a design lead at block (cash app, square, afterpay, tidal, bitkey, proto) collaborating to build and scale new ideas and systems that support economic empowerment.

he’s worked with a range of clients—including nike, meta, visa, robinhood, the exploratorium, and the san francisco symphony—earning international recognition for select projects.
